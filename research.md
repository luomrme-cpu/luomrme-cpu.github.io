# Research Interests

My research focuses on the intersection of **Finance**, **Capital Markets**, and **Health Insurance**. Below are my primary research areas:

---

## 💰 Financial Markets & Capital Markets

### Market Microstructure
- Analysis of market efficiency and liquidity
- Price formation and discovery mechanisms
- High-frequency trading impacts

### Asset Pricing
- Empirical asset pricing models
- Risk-return relationships
- Factor investing

### Corporate Finance
- Capital structure decisions
- Corporate governance mechanisms
- Investment policy and firm value

---

## 🏥 Health Insurance & Risk Management

### Insurance Economics
- Moral hazard and adverse selection in health insurance
- Insurance market design and regulation
- Risk pooling and diversification

### Healthcare Finance
- Financing mechanisms for healthcare systems
- Cost-effectiveness analysis
- Health expenditure and economic outcomes

### Intersection with Capital Markets
- How insurance markets interact with financial markets
- Capital requirements for insurance companies
- Investment behavior of insurance firms

---

## 📊 Applied Econometrics

- Microeconometric methods for causal inference
- Panel data analysis
- Time series analysis of financial markets
- Structural estimation in insurance research
