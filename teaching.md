## Teaching Philosophy 💡

I believe in active learning and connecting theoretical concepts to real-world applications. My classes emphasize:

- **Practical Examples**: Using real financial market data to illustrate theories
- **Critical Thinking**: Encouraging students to question and analyze assumptions
- **Hands-on Experience**: Incorporating empirical projects and data analysis
